About this site

A neighbor-built
directory

This directory started as a simple idea: when you move to a new neighborhood, finding trustworthy local businesses is harder than it should be. Word-of-mouth works — but only if you know who to ask.

Land O' Lakes Local is built to make that easier. It's a free, searchable guide to businesses that have a real connection to this community — people who live here, work here, or genuinely serve the people who do.

The site is curated and maintained by Jennifer Rose, a Connerton resident and local Realtor who spends a lot of time helping people get settled here. Connecting new neighbors with great local businesses felt like a natural extension of that.

When Jennifer isn't helping clients buy or sell in Land O' Lakes, she's probably at a Connerton community event — or looking up another small business to add to this list.

What makes this different

A directory with community ties

This isn't a platform where anyone can pay for placement. Every listing has been submitted by someone with a genuine connection to Land O' Lakes — and browsing or listing is always free.

🤝
Community connections
Businesses are listed because they have a real tie to the community — residents, local operators, and businesses that genuinely serve this area.
🔍
Useful filters
Quickly find veteran-owned businesses, first responder discounts, and Connerton residents. Search by name, category, or keyword.
🆓
Free to list, free to browse
There's no charge to submit a listing, and no charge to use the directory. This is a community resource — not a paid advertising platform.
